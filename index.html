OUR STORY

In July of 2011, Andrew met Kate at Valentino’s Italian Restaurant at a dinner hosted for the new cohort of UNL students who’d been admitted to the Raikes School of Computer Science and Management program. The two were off to a rocky start when Andrew mistakenly asked if Kate’s name was “Katie”. While Andrew proceeded to mentally beat himself up for the rest of the dinner, Kate completely forgot the 2-sentence conversation ever happened.

Kate met Andrew that August during the cohort-bonding activities on the first day of move-in, where Andrew’s 2nd 1st impression went a lot smoother. They hung out a lot during the first week, but by Friday Andrew’s paranoia about the chances of someone else asking Kate out before he did (with the 3:1 male-to-female ratio in Raikes) caused him to accelerate his plans. He asked Kate to go to the next day’s Husker game with him if she wasn’t going with anyone. Kate called her friends to apologize for ditching them and told Andrew she didn’t have any plans.

On a beautiful, post-graduation trip to Greece in May, 2015, Andrew proposed. Kate said yes!
